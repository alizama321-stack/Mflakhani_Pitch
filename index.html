Working Process

A transparent look at how we transform raw collections into retail-ready global shipments.

1. Warehouse Operations

Acquiring pristine credential materials directly from premium collection points.

2. Sorting Department

Initial separation of categories into clothing, footwear, accessories, and heavy apparel by trained specialists.

3. Quality Inspection

Detailed quality checks dividing textiles into distinct grades to meet exact wholesale market demands.

4. Packaging Process

High-density industrial baling presses items into compact waterproof blocks, maximizing freight volume.

5. Container Loading

Strategic stuffing of 20FT and 40FT HC containers by logistics experts prior to customs and maritime dispatch.
