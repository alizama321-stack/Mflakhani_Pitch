Meticulous Sorting Process

A transparent look at how we transform raw collections into retail-ready global shipments.

1. Raw Sourcing

Acquiring pristine, original credential donations directly from premium collection points in North America and Europe.

2. Quality Control Grading

Our specialists hand-sort textiles into over 150 distinct categories, strictly separating Grade A, B, and C items.

3. High-Density Baling

Apparel is processed through industrial balers, packing up to 100lbs (45kg) per block to maximize container freight efficiency.

4. Global Export

Containers are sealed, fumigated, and dispatched with full international customs documentation to wholesale partners.

Common Questions

Everything international wholesale buyers ask before placing their first container order.

Our minimum order is one full container load — either a 20FT (approximately 14,000 kg) or a 40FT (approximately 28,000 kg). We do not offer less-than-container (LCL) shipments for standard product lines. Mixed-product containers are available via our Shipment Configurator above.
All incoming donations are processed at our Karachi facility through a multi-stage grading system. Garments are classified into Grade A (retail-ready, minimal wear), Grade B (minor defects, suitable for resale markets), and Grade C (recycling/rag stream). Over 150 sub-categories are used to ensure precision sorting matched to each client's market requirements.
We export primarily on FOB (Free On Board) Karachi terms, with CIF (Cost, Insurance & Freight) available to major destination ports including Dubai (Jebel Ali), Mombasa, Rotterdam, Houston, and Santos. All shipments include phytosanitary fumigation and full export documentation compliant with destination country import regulations.
Standard lead time is 10–21 business days from deposit receipt to container loading, depending on product mix and availability. Custom-graded or specialty orders (e.g., specific brand vintage, single-category containers) may require up to 30 days. Clients are provided with a Bill of Lading within 5 days of vessel departure.
We accept Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) bank wire as our primary payment method. A 30–50% deposit is required to confirm your order and begin processing, with the balance due prior to container release. Letter of Credit (L/C) arrangements are available for established clients with a verified trading history.
Yes. We offer sample bales (approx. 45–100 kg) for new clients at a per-kg rate inclusive of courier shipping. Sample shipments allow you to assess grading quality and product mix before placing a full container order. Contact our team to discuss sample availability for your product category of interest.
